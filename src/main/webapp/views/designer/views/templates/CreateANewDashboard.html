Creating a new Dashboard

1. Click on the ‘New +’ button to create :

a) Workspace: A folder to save dashboards.

b) Dashboard: A place where design and development of dashboards takes place.


2. Click on ‘Dashboard’ button to create a dashboard.

New>Dashboard



3. After Clicking on the ‘Dashboard’, a web page will open in a new window.



4. Click on ‘Data Connectors’ icon provided to the right side of the canvas, and view the database connectivity options .

5. Click on the required connection type.



6 . Fill in the necessary details:

a) Name: Name the connection

b) Browse: Navigate to the Excel file that you wish to use as a data-source


7. Click on the 'Component Library', Drag and Drop any component.



8. Connect the chart to the created data connection by click on "Dataset" option that appears next to the each chart.



9 . Associating a connection to the dragged chart:

a) Select the connection from the ‘Connections’.

b) Drag and Drop the respected fields to ‘Series’ and ‘Category’.
