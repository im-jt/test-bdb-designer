Bullet Chart

Bullet charts provide 'rich display of data in a small space'. They are developed to replace the meters and gauges that are often used on the dashboards. Why? Because they won't provide enough information like that of bullet chart.

Bullet charts are used to compare one value, represented by a horizontal bar, to another value, represented by a vertical line, and relate those to qualitative ranges.

Best situation to use a Bullet Chart:

When to evaluate or compare performance of a primary metric (YTD revenue) compared to a target (annual revenue).

Variations of this chart :

Using colors at the backdrop of the chart helps users to quickly understand how the primary measure is performing against goals.
