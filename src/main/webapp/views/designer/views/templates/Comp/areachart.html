Area Chart

An area chart or area graph displays graphically quantitive data. It is based on the line chart. The primary use of area charts is to display trends over a period of time.

Best situation to use area chart:

Examples:

  • To view the automobile sales of a manufacturer over a period of time.
  • To view the revenue growth of an organization by quarter wise.
