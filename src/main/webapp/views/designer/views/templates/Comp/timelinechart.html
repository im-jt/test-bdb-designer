Timeline Chart

Timeline chart can be used to evaluate patterns and behavior in data over time. Also called 'Time Series' graphs. They show how values change over a time. Timeline charts display observations on the Y-axis against equally spaced time intervals on the X-axis.

Best situation to use a Timeline:

  • When comparing data patterns of different groups.
  • When examining daily, weekly, and annual variations of a product or an entity.
