Pie Chart

The most widely used chart to show proportions, percentages and categories information. Mainly used for comparison where the users can find out easily which category is most popular and which is least popular.

Best situation to use Pie Chart:

Mainly used for comparison of categories available on the pie To find out top performers and least performers. Better for viewing if the category is limited to 6 or fewer.

Variations of this chart:

Pie-Doughnut: Doughnut charts are similar to Pie-chart all you need to do is to switch the pie-chart to doughnut.
