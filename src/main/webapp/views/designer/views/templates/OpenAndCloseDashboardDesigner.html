How to open and close Dashboard Designer ?

Opening Dashboard Designer

i) Enter in the URL bar -->"https://app.bdb.ai/home/#/signin"

ii) Enter your credentials.

Fig: Login Page

iii) Click the ‘Login’ button. You will be taken to the Portal Home Screen.

iv) Click the ‘Apps’ icon to display a list of installed apps. Apps are BDB portal modules or plug-ins that provide specific functionality or can be used to extend the capabilities of BDB portal. Some apps come as standard with the BDB portal (ex. Administrator) and others must be licensed separately before they are made available in the portal.

Fig: Home Page

v) Select ‘Dashboard Designer’ (as shown above). Doing so will open a new webpage.

Fig: DesignerHome Page


Closing Dashboard Designer

Once you have completed creating a dashboard, save your work and use one of the following options to close the Designer.

i) From the Dashboard Designer home screen, click the ‘Exit’ icon.

ii) Click the ‘Close’ button (the ‘X’ on the right edge) of the Dashboard Designer tab.
