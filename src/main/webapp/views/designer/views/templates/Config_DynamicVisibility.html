Dynamic Visibility

1. Drag 2 charts and assign data to it.

Note: In this case, we are selecting Pie chart and Column chart.


2. For Column Chart, click on properties icon which appears next to chart. In General,disable Initial Visibility.


3. Drag a Label component available in Other tab of Component Library.


4. Click on 'Script on Load' which appears next to the label component.


5. Apply show and hide scripts.


6. Preview the dashboard, it will display only Pie chart


7. Click on 'Label', then it will show the Column chart and hide Pie chart
